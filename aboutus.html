Origin · 2024

Zerionx emerged from a simple observation: the gap between institutional-grade probability models and independent traders keeps widening. Most tools sell certainty — “target price”, “bullish breakout”, “tomorrow’s top pick”.

We don’t. We build risk estimation engines that output skew, regime likelihood, and volatility bands. No advice, no custody, no performance marketing. Three years of prototyping with Indian quant shops and crypto market makers shaped the current stack.
